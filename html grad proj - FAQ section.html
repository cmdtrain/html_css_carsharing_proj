FAQ — Answering Your Questions

Carsharing gives you access to vehicles on demand. Simply register, book via the app or website, pick up the car at the designated location, drive, then return it when you're done.

Costs typically include a usage fee (hourly/daily), a per‑kilometer charge, and sometimes a small membership or booking fee. All charges are clearly shown before you confirm your booking.

Only licensed, registered members on your account can drive. Sharing your key or app access with non‑members is not allowed.

You can cancel without fee up to 12 hours before the booking starts. Late cancellations or no‑shows may incur a small penalty — see our full terms for details.
